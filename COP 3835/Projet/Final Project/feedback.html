My feebacks after 4 months.
After a semester at FIU, I can already make a first statement about the courses, the life here.
First of, the administrativ part. It took some times to get my VISA and the other documents but once it was done everything went well.
Same goes for finding a place to stay, it was one of the hardest thing to do since it was really late with covid.

Finally the courses, it took again some times to find some, but with the help of FIU found 4 nice courses, even tho they end up being pretty easy.

Finally, living here is really awesome, I feel like its the same as Paris in better lel.
